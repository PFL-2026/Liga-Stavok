PFL × Liga Stavok Partnership

2026–2027 · Russia

Partnership Framework

Term
2-year partnership (2026–2027)
  • Year 1: Effective date – 31 December 2026
  • Year 2: 1 January 2027 – 31 December 2027
Franchises & Events
2026–2027: PFL Global Events
  • A minimum of 10 events in 2026
  • A minimum of 16 events per year from 2027
Territory
Russia
Financial commitment
  • 2026: $300,000
  • 2027: $425,000
Territory event uplift
2026–2028
  • Applicable to any event hosted within the Territory during the partnership term.
  • Liga Stavok to pay $60,000 per event, inclusive of all rights and deliverables outlined in Point 5 below.
  • Opportunity to upgrade to Presenting Partner status, subject to separate negotiation.

Partnership Assets

01

Trademark Rights & Official Designations

  • Use of PFL official marks across all marketing platforms.
  • 2026–2027: "Exclusive Betting Partner for PFL in Russia" or other mutually agreed designation.
  • Non-exclusive designations: "Official Partner of PFL".
02

Broadcast Integrations

  • Liga Stavok to receive access to custom broadcast opportunities per event. Examples may include:
    • Liga Stavok watermark logo feature throughout broadcast
    • Promotional billboards
    • Lower-third on-screen / Fight Presenter / Tale of the Tape graphic
    • Live odds integration
  • Above are subject to approvals with broadcaster.
  • All broadcast display limited to feeds distributed solely within Russia.
03

Virtual Overlay Branding

  • Maximum of 4 out-of-territory events with virtual logo placement on 1 large canvas, 1 vertical bumper and 1 inner middle canvas position.
  • Any additional events beyond the initial 4 will be charged at $15,000 per event.
  • Events to be selected by Liga Stavok.
  • All broadcast display limited to feeds distributed solely within Russia.
04

Social Content Distribution

  • A total of 3 social media posts per event.
  • Logo inclusion and tag of Liga Stavok on each post.
  • Liga Stavok and PFL to mutually agree upon 2 custom sports betting content series per year, included within social posts allocation.
  • Liga Stavok's logo to be featured on the Sponsor and How to Bet section of PFLmma.com.
05

Assets Included for Any Event In-Territory

  • 1× large canvas
  • 1× vertical bumper
  • 1× additional Full Apron Placement on cage
  • Minimum of 8 posts from official PFL social channels to be presented by Liga Stavok, within one month of each event — posts to be mutually agreed.
06

PFL Digital & Video Content

  • PFL will provide pre-, during-, and post-event content assets, including but not limited to:
    • Pre-event: press conferences, weigh-ins, face-offs, promos, fighter media-day cutdowns
    • During event: rapid-turn highlight cutdowns
    • Post-event: official photos and recap videos
  • All content may be used across Liga Stavok-owned channels, with specified rules.
07

Watch & Bet / Data

  • Liga Stavok will receive non-exclusive live event Watch & Bet rights during the Term (e.g., live event stream for distribution on Brand platforms).
  • Liga Stavok will receive the official PFL data feed, to the extent available, via Sportradar.
08

Athlete Ambassador Program

  • Liga Stavok will receive 2 fighters across the PFL roster to be Liga Stavok ambassadors for each year.
  • The PFL will work in conjunction with Liga Stavok to define the roles & responsibilities for these athletes, which can include but not be limited to:
    • Digital content support
    • Fight kit logo inclusion
    • Consumer-facing programs (appearances / autograph signings)
    • Other roles and responsibilities as mutually agreed
09

VIP Hospitality & Access
(per event)

  • 3× VIP tickets
  • 3× GA tickets
  • Additional ideas may include:
    • On-stage + meet-and-greet at weigh-ins
    • Hosted backstage tour during fight night
    • Invitation to press conferences, weigh-ins, face-offs
10

Account Management

  • For each year of the partnership, the PFL will provide Liga Stavok with an Account Director / point-of-contact, for turnkey execution of the partnership.
